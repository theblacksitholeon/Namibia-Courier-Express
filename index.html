    WHO WE ARE

    RHONAFLO FOUNDATION is an organization registered and operating in accordance with the laws of Zimbabwe (MA 000/0275). It was founded in 2018. We implement programs that promote and enhance safe births in Zimbabwe, while also amplifying sexual and reproductive health knowledge for women and girls.

    RHONAFLO supports maternal health in Zimbabwe through the following:

    • ● Providing support pre, during and post-delivery to mothers in need to improve and enhance their maternal health outcomes
    • ● Acquisition of maternal care kits (APONA Hampers) to distribute to pregnant mothers in need. Kits have both clinical and non-clinical components required during and after the birthing process.
    • ● Maternal health education
    • ● Counselling services
    • ● Mobilization of resources from various stakeholders worldwide with proceeds channelled to supporting vulnerable pregnant women in Zimbabwe. Support is provided pre, during and post-delivery.

    The Foundation uses strategic program implementation with advocacy-based messaging in our work. This is to encourage and incentivise positive health seeking behaviour amongst pregnant women in resource-poor settings through working hand in hand with various key stakeholders in communities. RHONAFLO programs occur at community-level. This occurrence consists of partnerships with clinics, community leadership, Department of Social Service, Village health workers and maternity waiting homes to improve maternal conditions at this primary care level. We also work with 29 Village Health workers in Mazowe District, Mashonaland Central. Through their community-based visits, they disseminate key health information on our behalf, identify at risk and vulnerable pregnant mothers and monitor pregnant and nursing mothers: carrying out duties such as measuring and monitoring baby growth.

    OUR VISION

    A Zimbabwe that has no mother or child who dies from preventable maternal-related causes.

    OUR MISSION

    To ensure that women have access to quality, respectful and equitable maternity care by collaborating with health facilities and the community at large to provide solutions and incentives that encourage health seeking behaviour from women and girls. The organization aims to do this by:

    • Identifying and implementing practical and cost-effective interventions that reduce maternal mortality and morbidity.
    • Facilitating women’s access to high quality, affordable, safe, respectful, and skilled maternity care.
    • Increasing economic opportunities for pregnant women. RHONAFLO believes that a woman with self-sufficient economic means is better able to make positive and independent decisions over their health and wellbeing and have agency over their lives.

    OUR GOALS

    1) Quality Maternal Care provision, and

    2) Empowerment of Expecting mothers, as an approach for realizing RHONAFLO’s vision and achieving its mission.

    Goal 1

    Quality Maternal Care provision

    Objective: Develop and maintain partnerships with communities and health facilities to: I. Mobilise communities and pregnant mothers to seek and expand use of health facilities for ante-natal and post-natal care II. Develop and implement the APONA Hamper Initiative to incentivize pregnant mothers to seek maternal care at health facilities III. Expand the level of advocacy and health education communication strategies and capabilities with regards to maternal health at community level

    The APONA Hamper is an incentive package of key birth associated components provided to expectant mothers in need to encourage facility-based safe and quality childbirth.
